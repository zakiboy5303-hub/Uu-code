The Activities at pacific trails

Pacific Trails Resort offers a variety of activities for guests who love adventure and relaxation.

  • Enjoy guided hiking tours along the scenic coastal trails
  • Experience kayaking and ocean exploration with our expert guides
  • Join our yoga and wellness sessions held daily
  • Discover local wildlife and nature photography opportunities
  • Take part in evening campfire gatherings and storytelling
